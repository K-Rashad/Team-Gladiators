GOVERNMENT ENGINEERING COLLEGE THRISSUR

About GECT

Government Engineering College Thrissur (GEC Thrissur) formerly Government Engineering College Trichur, an ISO 9001: 2008 certified institution is one of the most prestigious and reputed technical education institution in south India. The College was established in the year 1957. The foundation stone of the college Main Building was laid down by the then Hon. Prime Minister of India, Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru. The college campus spread over an area of more than 75 acres is located at Ramavarmapuram, about 5 km from Thrissur city, 6 km from the Thrissur railway station and about 58 km from the Cochin Airport.

All the departments of the college are adequately staffed and well equipped to meet the needs of latest technological trends and International standards. The college has 47447.57 sq.m. of built up area for academic and administration purposes and houses facilities for arts, sports and games. The college campus houses a separate library block, a central computing facility (CCF), a state of the art auditorium (Eastern Amphi Theatre), hostel blocks for both male and female, recreational facilities, food court, dispensary, bank and a post office. The millennium auditorium meets the requirement of all cultural activities of the students and staff and other major events organized in the college. The college is one of the top most institutions in Kerala with regard to the placement opportunities by consistently offering near 100% placement during the recent years.

GEC Thrissur has a substantial number of distinguished alumni and the college is extremely proud of all its alumni. Most of the alumni are executives in various organizations and entrepreneurs both within India and abroad. A significant number of them are holding prestigious positions like Chief Executive or Managing Director or Chairmen in various organizations all over the world.
